First meet

Aug 29 2015

It was no different from any other night, almost. Brittany and Michael both had plans with their friends to go out for the night. Both of their friends ended up at Joe's Brewery. It was there where Brittany and Michael met. Michael noticed Brittany our of the corner of his eye and he knew something was different about this girl. He then approached her and they danced the night away. When Joe's closed Brittany and Michael continued on with their night. They stopped stop to get Brittany's favorite food, chicken nuggets. They then stayed up all night talking about their hopes and futures. On that night, Brittany and Michael knew that this night was different from others and they were mean't to be together forever.
